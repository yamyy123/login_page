Parrot

Parrots are colorful birds known for their intelligence and ability to mimic human speech. They are popular pets and are found in various species worldwide.

Finch

Finches are small, seed-eating birds known for their cheerful songs. They come in a variety of colors and are often kept as pets for their lively nature.

Lutino Cockatiels

Lutino cockatiels are vibrant birds with yellow plumage, orange cheek patches, and red eyes. Known for their friendly nature, they make popular and charming pets.

Chick

Chicks, with fluffy downy feathers, represent the early stage of bird development. As they mature, their appearance transforms, promising the potential for colorful beauty.
